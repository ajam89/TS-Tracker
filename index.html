Implemented Rules Logic

This build follows your final rules set: Iran is excluded from USSR adjacency, Japan counts as adjacent to the USA, Canada scores inside Europe while still granting USA adjacency points to the USSR, and Presence / Domination / Control do not stack; only the highest achieved level applies for each side in each region.
Presence: Control at least 1 country in the region.
Domination: More countries than the opponent + more Battlegrounds than the opponent + at least 1 non-Battleground.
Control: All Battlegrounds in the region + more countries than the opponent.
Final Score: Highest Level Only + Battleground Bonus + Adjacency Bonus.
